# Technical Steering Committee (TSC) Policy

**Version 1.0** — April 2026

## 1. Purpose
The TSC is responsible for day-to-day technical direction and governance of AIC/HMN while preserving the Founder's strategic oversight and the project's core principles.

## 2. Principles
- Founder-Led Driven: The Founder retains ultimate strategic authority.
- Merit-based + Alignment: Membership is earned through contribution and demonstrated commitment to Third Path Absolute.
- Transparency: All TSC decisions (except sensitive security) are public.

## 3. Composition
- 3–5 members including the Founder (permanent member).
- At least 2 members must be external contributors.

## 4. Responsibilities
- Define and maintain technical roadmap for individual modules.
- Review and merge critical PRs.
- Issue triage and prioritization.
- Propose improvements to architecture and governance.
- Represent the project in technical discussions.

## 5. Decision Making
- Core principles (ethical kernel, zero-donation, Third Path) require Founder's explicit approval.
- Technical decisions use majority vote.
- Deadlock → Founder decides.

## 6. Membership Criteria
- Minimum 9–12 months of consistent, high-quality contributions.
- Public commitment to AIC's core principles.
- Strong technical skills and judgment.
- No major conflict of interest with Big Tech frontier labs.

## 7. Appointment & Removal
- Appointed by Founder after TSC internal vote.
- Can be removed by 2/3 TSC vote + Founder approval.

---

**Commitment**: The TSC exists to support, not replace, the Founder's vision.
— Nguyễn Đức Trí, Founder
