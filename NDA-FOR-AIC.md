# NDA for AIC (Governance & Financial Scope)

## Purpose
This Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) applies **only** to governance discussions and financial information managed through Open Collective (OCE).  
It does **not** apply to code contributions, repositories, or any open-source materials licensed under GPL-3.0 and Open MDW 2025.

## Scope of Confidentiality
1. **Governance Discussions**  
   Internal deliberations, decisions, or strategic planning related to AIC governance that are not yet public must remain confidential.

2. **Financial Information**  
   Budgets, donations, expenses, and financial allocations within Open Collective are confidential unless officially published.

3. **Exclusions**  
   Code contributions, technical documentation, and open-source materials are excluded from this NDA and remain fully transparent.

## Obligations
- Contributors agree not to disclose, share, or publish confidential governance or financial information without explicit permission from the Founder Admin.  
- Any breach of confidentiality may result in removal from the contributor group and revocation of privileges.

## Duration
This NDA remains in effect during the contributor’s involvement with AIC and for **12 months after departure**.

## Founder Authority
The Founder Admin retains sole discretion to:
- Designate additional admins on OCE.  
- Determine which governance or financial information is classified as confidential.

---

**Note**: This NDA is intentionally narrow in scope to preserve the open-source spirit of AIC while protecting sensitive governance and financial matters.
