# Governance Model

**Adaptive Intelligence Circle (AIC) & Human Meaning Network (HMN)** is a founder-led, community-supported, non-profit open-source project.

**Decision-making structure:**
- **Founder (Nguyễn Đức Trí)** holds final authority on vision, ethical direction, and core architecture.
- **Open Collective** serves as fiscal sponsor and legal entity.
- **Core Maintainers / Admins** (invited by Founder) handle day-to-day operations of public repositories.
- All major changes to core principles (zero-donation, third path, ethical kernel) require explicit Founder approval.

During the Founder’s mandatory military service (2027–2029), the project enters **maintenance mode**. No new core development is allowed until the Founder returns.
