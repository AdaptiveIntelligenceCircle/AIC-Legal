## 1. Purpose

This document outlines Adaptive Intelligence Circle (AIC) and Human Meaning Network (HMN)'s strategy for complying with the **EU Artificial Intelligence Act** while preserving our core principles:  
- **Third Path Absolute**  
- **Zero-Donation Policy**  
- **Ethical-from-Kernel** design  
- Complete independence from any government or corporate influence

We treat EU AI Act compliance as a **legal and operational necessity**, not a philosophical compromise.

## 3. Compliance Strategy

AIC adopts a **minimal yet transparent compliance** approach:

1. **Transparency-First**  
   - All high-risk components will be documented publicly in this repository.  
   - Technical documentation, risk assessments, and conformity processes will be made available via GitHub.

2. **Human Oversight & Ethical Safeguards**  
   - Maintain **Introspective Behavioral Control System (IBCS)** as the ethical core.  
   - Implement mandatory human-in-the-loop for critical decisions where required by the Act.

3. **Research-Oriented Development**  
   - Prioritize research, prototyping, and open-source experimentation over commercial deployment in the EU.  
   - Any production deployment in EU territory will undergo full conformity assessment before release.

4. **Data & Privacy Protection**  
   - Strict adherence to GDPR alongside EU AI Act.  
   - No training on personal data without explicit, revocable consent.

5. **No Compromise on Founding Principles**  
   - Zero-donation policy remains absolute.  
   - No funding, partnership, or influence from EU institutions that would undermine Third Path independence.  
   - Compliance is accepted only to the minimum extent required by law.

## 4. Governance Commitment

- We commit to full transparency: all conformity documents will be open-source unless prohibited by law.
