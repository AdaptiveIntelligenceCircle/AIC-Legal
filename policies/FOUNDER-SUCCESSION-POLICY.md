# Founder Succession Policy - Adaptive Intelligence Circle (AIC) & Human Meaning Network (HMN)

**Version 1.0** — April 2026  
**Maintained by**: Founder Nguyễn Đức Trí  
**License**: GPLv3 + OpenMDW-1.0 Supplement

## 1. Purpose
This policy ensures the long-term survival and integrity of AIC/HMN's core principles (Third Path Absolute, zero-donation, ethical-from-kernel) even in the absence or reduced capacity of the Founder.

## 2. Principles
- The Founder retains strategic veto power on core direction as long as he is able and willing.
- Succession is **not** a transfer of ownership, but a **distributed stewardship** model.
- Any successor or committee **must** uphold zero-donation and Third Path Absolute without exception.
- Emergency succession and planned succession are handled differently.

## 3. Emergency Succession (Sudden Incapacity)
- In case the Founder becomes unavailable (health, military service, force majeure), an **Emergency Steering Group** of 2–3 Trusted Admins (already designated) will temporarily maintain the project.
- They can only perform:
  - Security patches
  - Bug fixes
  - Governance maintenance
  - Public communication (with transparency)
- They **cannot** change license, accept funding with strings, or alter ethical kernel direction.

## 4. Planned Long-term Succession
When the Founder decides to step back from day-to-day leadership (after 2029 or later):

- A **Technical Steering Committee (TSC)** will be formed with 3–5 members.
- Selection criteria for TSC members:
  - Proven long-term contribution (minimum 12 months consistent in-kind contribution).
  - Public commitment to Third Path Absolute and zero-donation.
  - Demonstrated understanding of ethical-from-kernel philosophy.
- Founder retains **permanent veto right** on any change that affects core principles.

## 5. Knowledge Transfer & Continuity
- All critical knowledge must be documented in the `AIC-Legal-and-Governance` repository.
- Regular "bus factor" exercises (at least once per year).
- Founder will prepare a "Founder Handover Manual" covering vision, non-negotiable principles, and decision-making philosophy.

## 6. Amendment
This policy can only be amended with explicit approval from the Founder while he is active, or by unanimous vote of the TSC + public 30-day comment period after the Founder steps back.
