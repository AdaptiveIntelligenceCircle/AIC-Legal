# Reputation & In-Kind Contribution System (AIC)

**Philosophy**: We reward **impact** and **alignment with principles**, not hours or code volume.

## Contribution Categories (In-kind only)
- Code & Technical (bug fixes, features, tests, architecture)
- Documentation & Tutorials
- Governance & Policy improvement
- Community support (issue triage, reviews, mentoring)
- Research & Analysis (geopolitics, ethics, long-term vision)
- Security & Recovery contributions
- Translation & Outreach (without compromising Third Path)

## Reputation Levels (Merit-based)
- **Observer**: Newcomer
- **Contributor**: ≥ 3 meaningful contributions + passed basic alignment check
- **Active Builder**: Consistent contribution (≥ 6 months) + high-quality reviews
- **Steward**: Can be invited to Trusted Admin or TSC (requires deep understanding of ethical kernel)

## Recognition Mechanisms (không tiền)
- Public shout-out in monthly Status Update
- Co-author on technical documents or research notes
- "Impact Badge" trên GitHub profile (custom via discussion)
- Priority review for their PRs
- Mention in GOVERNANCE.md or annual report
- Opportunity to co-lead small modules (with Founder oversight)

## Anti-Gaming Rules
- Contributions must align with Third Path and zero-donation.
- Spam, low-effort, or self-promotion → negative reputation.
- Final decision on reputation belongs to Founder (or TSC later).

This system is designed to attract people who truly believe in the mission, not those seeking financial or status rewards.
