# Ethical Use Policy

AIC/HMN is built with the principle **Ethical-from-Kernel**.  
Any use, fork, or derivative work must respect the following:

1. The project must remain non-profit and zero-donation.
2. Ethical behavior must be embedded at the system/kernel level, not only at the application layer.
3. No use for surveillance, military targeting, manipulation of public opinion, or harm to human autonomy.
4. Transparency and third-path independence must be maintained.

Violation of this policy may result in public revocation of permission to use the name “AIC” or “HMN”.
