# Contributor & Maintainer Agreement

By contributing or becoming a maintainer, you agree to:
1. Respect all core principles (zero-donation, third path, ethical-from-kernel).
2. Not accept any donation or sponsorship on behalf of the project.
3. Not close-source or relicense any part of the core without Founder approval.
4. If the Founder takes military service (2027–2029), only perform maintenance on public repositories.

Violation may result in immediate removal of access.
