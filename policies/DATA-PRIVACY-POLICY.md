# Data Privacy Policy

**Version 1.0** — April 2026

## 1. Philosophy
AIC is built on the principle of **Self-Sovereign Identity** and human autonomy. We minimize data collection by design and reject surveillance capitalism.

## 2. Data We Collect
- **Minimal by default**: We do not collect personal data unless absolutely necessary for the function of the system.
- Technical logs (anonymized when possible)
- Contribution data (GitHub username, PRs, issues) for reputation system
- Data explicitly provided by users in SSI or Recovery modules (user-controlled)

## 3. Key Principles
- **No tracking** of individual users across systems without explicit consent.
- **User data belongs to the user**: In SSI modules, users retain full control over their identity data.
- **No selling or sharing** data with third parties.
- **Zero-donation** also means we do not monetize user data.

## 4. Data Retention
- Contribution metadata is kept for transparency.
- Sensitive user data in Recovery/SSI systems is encrypted and user-managed.
- Logs are kept only as long as necessary for security and debugging.

## 5. User Rights
Users have the right to:
- Access their data
- Request deletion (where technically feasible)
- Export their data

## 6. Compliance
We aim to align with GDPR principles and best practices in data minimization, even though we are a small independent NPO.
