## 1. Purpose

This document declares Adaptive Intelligence Circle (AIC) and Human Meaning Network (HMN)'s official position regarding compliance with EU regulations (including the EU AI Act) in the context of the **Vietnam – EU Comprehensive Strategic Partnership** officially upgraded in 2026.

We fully recognize and respect Vietnam’s foreign policy of elevating relations with the European Union to a Comprehensive Strategic Partnership. At the same time, we maintain our founding principles without compromise:
- **Third Path Absolute**
- **Zero-Donation Policy**
- **Ethical-from-Kernel** design
- Complete independence from any government or corporate influence

Compliance with EU law is accepted only to the extent necessary for legal transparency and international cooperation, never at the cost of AIC’s core independence.

## 2. Context: Vietnam – EU Comprehensive Strategic Partnership (2026)

According to the official announcement by the Vietnamese Government, Vietnam and the European Union have upgraded their relationship to a **Comprehensive Strategic Partnership** in 2026. This partnership emphasizes:
- Deepening cooperation in science, technology, innovation and digital transformation
- Promoting ethical and responsible use of emerging technologies
- Strengthening people-to-people ties and sustainable development

AIC, as a project initiated by a Vietnamese founder, views this partnership as a positive framework for international collaboration while preserving national technological sovereignty and independent innovation.

## 3. AIC’s Compliance Position

1. **Alignment with Vietnam’s Foreign Policy**  
   AIC supports Vietnam’s strategy of comprehensive international cooperation. Our decision to seek fiscal hosting in Europe is consistent with this elevated partnership and is not an act of alignment with any single bloc.

2. **Minimal & Transparent Compliance**  
   AIC will comply with applicable EU regulations (including the EU AI Act) only to the minimum extent required by law, while:
   - Maintaining full transparency through public documentation
   - Preserving zero-donation and Third Path principles
   - Ensuring all high-risk components (especially AIC-SSI) undergo necessary conformity processes without compromising ethical-from-kernel architecture

3. **No Compromise on Independence**  
   - AIC does not accept any funding, direction, or influence from EU institutions, governments, or corporations.
   - Compliance is technical and legal, never ideological or political.
   - We remain a Vietnamese-initiated, independently governed project.

## 4. Governance Commitment

- This strategy will be reviewed every six months or upon any significant change in Vietnam–EU relations or EU AI Act implementation.
- Any updates will be announced publicly in the ANNOUNCEMENTS folder of this repository.
- We commit to full openness: all compliance-related documents will be published unless explicitly prohibited by law.
